About Us

We are a group of local cycling enthusiasts who meet up every Sunday at 2pm for recreational bike rides, barring bank holidays or seriously bad weather. We have three groups, one each for easy, intermediate and challenging rides, check out the meeting info below. Also on this website you'll find some popular local routes, a route planner and a map with locations of local bike shops. Feel free to come along to any of our meet-ups. If you would like more info then please send a message.

Cycling Meet-Ups:

Sundays at 2pm

Easy Riders
