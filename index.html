About Us

We are a local cycling club, based in Bedford who meet up every Sunday at 2pm for social, non-competetive bike rides, barring national holidays or seriously bad weather.

We have three groups, one each for easy, intermediate and more challenging rides, check out the meeting info below.

We also hold club events, including an annual charity ride and occasional social outings.

On this website you will also find a photo gallery, some recommended routes, a route planner, and a map with locations of local bike shops.

Feel free to come along to any of our meet-ups. If you would like more info then please send a message.
