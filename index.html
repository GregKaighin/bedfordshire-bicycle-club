About Us

We are a group of enthusiastic local cyclists who meet regularly for recreational rides. We have three groups, one each for easy, intermediate and challenging rides. Check out the meeting info below if you'd like to join us. Also on this website you'll find some popular local routes, a route planner and map with locations of local bike shops. Feel free to come along to any of our meet-ups. If you would like more info please send a message.

Meets

Easy Riders

A gentle ride of no more than 5 miles, at a nice and easy pace.

Middle of the Road

A ride of 5-10 miles at a moderate pace. Perfect for intermediate riders.

Pedal Pushers

A longer ride of 10-20 miles to suit more advanced cyclists.
